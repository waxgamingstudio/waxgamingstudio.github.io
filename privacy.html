Privacy Policy

At Wax Gaming, we respect your privacy. Here's a simple overview of how your data is managed.

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Wax Gaming. This Privacy Policy explains how we operate our mobile games and handle any data that may be collected in connection with their use. This policy applies to all games and applications published by Wax Gaming on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

We do not collect personal data directly. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information through our games. Our apps are designed to work without requiring you to create an account or share personal details with us.

By downloading or using any of our games you acknowledge the practices described in this policy.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Our games may integrate third-party advertising and analytics SDKs to support free gameplay and improve the app experience. These third-party services operate independently and are governed by their own privacy policies. The services we may use include:

Google AdMob 👉 Privacy Policy

AdMob by Google serves in-app advertisements. It may collect device information, advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID), IP address, and usage data to display personalised or contextual ads.

Unity Ads 👉 Privacy Policy

Unity Ads provides rewarded video and interstitial advertisements. Unity may collect device identifiers (IDFA / GAID), gameplay data, and advertising IDs to optimise ad delivery and relevance.

Google Firebase 👉 Privacy Policy

Firebase is used for app analytics and crash reporting. It may collect device information, app session data, crash logs, and aggregated usage statistics to help us improve stability and performance.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information on how they collect, use, and protect your data.

🗄️ Data Collection

Developer collection: Wax Gaming does not directly collect, store, or process any personal data from users of our games. We do not operate any servers that receive or retain personal user information.

Third-party SDK collection: The third-party SDKs listed above may automatically collect certain technical and usage data when you use our games. This may include:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • IP address and general location (country/region level)
  • In-app events, session duration, and feature usage
  • App crash reports and performance diagnostics

This data is collected and processed by the respective third parties under their own privacy policies. Wax Gaming only receives aggregated, anonymised analytics summaries.

🧒 Children's Privacy

Wax Gaming is committed to protecting the privacy of children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provisions for children, and applicable guidelines set by the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

We do not knowingly or intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Our apps do not contain forms or prompts that request personal data from any user, including children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has shared personal information via a third-party SDK, we recommend contacting the relevant third-party provider directly, as Wax Gaming does not manage or control their data practices.

📢 Advertising & Your Choices

Our free games are supported by advertising provided by third-party ad networks (AdMob, Unity Ads). These networks may use advertising identifiers to deliver relevant ads.

You can limit interest-based advertising or reset your advertising identifier at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

Please note that opting out of personalised ads does not remove advertising from our games; you may still see contextual (non-personalised) ads.

🔒 Security

Protecting user data is important to us. Although we do not store personal data on our own servers, we take reasonable precautions to ensure that any data handled within our apps and by our integrated third-party SDKs is managed responsibly.

We select third-party partners that maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data processed by third parties.
